Special Smoothie Recipe

If you want to have a healthy smoothie, you are at the right stop. This smoothie recipe contains no sweetner. We will only work with the sweetness of the fruits involved. And above all, it's completely healthy.

Recipe Information: All you need to know.

  • Preparation time: 5 mins
  • Cooking time: 2 mins
  • Number of servings: 1
  • Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients: All the ingredients you know.

This recipe is mine, but you can - Click here to see more smoothie recipes

  • Unsweetened Greek Yoghurt
  • Frozen Banana
  • Strawberries
  • Pineapples
  • Oranges
  • Red Apple
  • Complan
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Rinse your blender
  2. Add 4 spoons of the unsweetened yoghurt to the blender.
  3. Add one finger of frozen slices of banana.
  4. Add the sliced red apple(take out the seeds).
  5. Add your sliced strawberries.
  6. Add the sliced Pineapples.
  7. Add the squeezed orange juice(Ensure that there are no seeds inside).
  8. Mix 4 spoons of the complan in water.
  9. Then blend everything together until you get your desired consistency.
  10. Serve.

This is a picture of what your finished product should look like:

Note:

It is important to ensure that all seeds are removed from the fruits before adding to the blender and eventually starting the blender.
This is to avoid spoiling your blender. As some of the seeds may be too hard to blend along. This doesn't apply to all fruits, but for this particular recipe, it's important.
Also remember to wash your blender before and after use.
